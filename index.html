About
Pooja krishna.

Greetings, fellow dreamers! I'm Pooja Krishna, a storyteller by heart and an artist by soul, navigating the enchanting landscapes of the entertainment world. From the whimsical stages of Variety Media, Jango Space, and Behindwoods to the captivating frames of short films and feature films like 'Rekha,' I've woven my narrative with threads of passion and creativity. As an anchor, I'm the conductor of emotions, orchestrating moments that linger in hearts. Interviews with Malayalam luminaries on YouTube have been my canvas for exploring the depths of personalities and tales.

Join me in this kaleidoscopic journey where every role, every word, and every frame tells a unique story—a story we create together in the grand tapestry of life!"
